How to answer the pairwise comparisons

A quick guide to the sliders in the survey, and why each set of three answers needs to work together.

1. Try the slider

Drag it left or right. The further you drag, the stronger your preference. Leave it in the middle if both matter equally.

2. Why the three answers in a group need to agree

3. Before moving to the next page

  1. Look at your three answers together, not just one at a time.
  2. Check that the one you rated highest overall is consistent across all three comparisons.
  3. If not, adjust one of the sliders so the order makes sense.
